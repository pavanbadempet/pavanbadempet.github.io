Pavan Badempet is a **Data Engineer** based in Hyderabad, India, with 2+ years of professional experience at **Tata Consultancy Services**. He specializes in building large-scale data pipelines, cloud-native architectures, and production-grade AI/ML systems.

At TCS, Pavan has worked across FinTech (Nomura Capital) and Automotive (Nissan) domains — engineering Spark ETL pipelines for capital markets datasets, migrating workloads from YARN to Kubernetes, and architecting serverless batch pipelines on AWS Lambda and Step Functions.

His technical toolkit spans **Python, Apache Spark, SQL, AWS, Snowflake, Docker, and FastAPI**. He holds a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (AI/ML minor) from Guru Nanak Institutions Technical Campus with an 8.2 CGPA and was awarded the Prime Minister's Scholarship 2019–2023.

Outside of work, Pavan builds open-source projects at the intersection of data engineering and AI — including a multi-disease healthcare prediction platform and a semantic movie recommendation engine using FAISS and sentence embeddings.

He is open to full-time, contract, and freelance opportunities in data engineering, MLOps, and cloud data platform roles.
